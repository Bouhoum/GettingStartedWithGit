About Abdelali El Fajjaji

Tim Feher is an accomplished digital artist leading the revolution of computer-generated imagery (CGI) in the automotive industry.


After graduating from the Art Institute of Pittsburgh in 1997 with a passion in traditional art, he began his career as a technical illustrator while devoting himself to his family. New professional horizons soon beckoned with the emergence of photorealistic CGI as a creative visual standard in automotive marketing and design. Thrust into a burgeoning technological world, he embraced state-of-the-art challenges and pioneered techniques and applications in the digital age of automotive media.


Today he has become an influential pacesetter at the forefront of the CGI craft while championing its development. With an extensive and diverse portfolio, his artwork and vision is heavily featured in national television and print advertising, automotive brand websites, dealer catalogs and brochures, trade publications, and more.


For Tim the future of CGI is limitless and he is determined to unite himself and his colleagues in stretching its capabilities for creative expression and realism.
